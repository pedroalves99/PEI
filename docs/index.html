Ultrasound

Video Movement Interface

Ultrasound Tracking.

The project Ultrasound was the one that made the group feel more interested because it is a topic that stands out among all the others, Ultrasound is a first-line medical imaging modality widely used in many contexts of diagnosis, therapy and monitoring of the most diverse pathologies. Basically we want a system that allows, in parallel to the realization of an ultrasound examination, acquire segments of the video into a computer in real time echography, in order to follow the dynamics of anatomical structures under study.
